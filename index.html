About us

We are a group of 12 students in the robotics and dependable system fields. Together we have embarked on an exciting journey towards the design of an intelligent gripper. This gripper will be able to grasp objects of unknown shape, size, and weight, something that is not available today in the industry.

Introducing SlipNot

SliptNot is an intelligent gripper that can detect unknown objects, determine the shape and size of the object, and decide how to grasp it in the most optimal way. All this is done by the gripper’s onboard computers, meaning that no external input is needed. The gripper will be designed towards industrial collaborative environments to work closely with humans. SlipNot is a step towards the future of industrial robotic applications.
